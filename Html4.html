Project 原子宇宙 (Project GenUch-o)

A Very Large Multiverse

A collective multiverse for building and telling fantasy stories.

GenUch-o is a Fantasy based world filled with Original characters by a collection of authors and artists. These works all happen in a singular multiverse, ranging from slice-of-life all the way to giant robots destroying existence. Most of these stories are all aligned by the deities that have built these worlds and the singular energy source used by all living things in them, known as Zen.
